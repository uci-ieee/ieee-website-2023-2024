Microchip Technology Info Session

Wednesday, May 17 12:30PM-2:00PM

At 12:30 - 2:00 pm, there is a Microchip Technology Info Session at the Division of Career Pathways Training Room. Here, you can join the networking event with Microchip Technology, a leading supplier of embedded control solutions that deliver a broad range of innovative products, including specialized microcontrollers, mixed-signal interfaces, and many other devices. At this event, Microchip will provide insights into their company and you'll also have the opportunity to meet their hiring managers. This event is in collaboration with IEEE at UCI and the Division of Career Pathways and is open to all students!

Working Professionals Panel

Monday, Nov 7th 7:30 - 8:30PM

IEEE at UCI is hosting its very first Working Professionals' Panel this Thursday from 5-7pm at EH 2430. Topics of the evening include: Semiconductors, IoT, interviewing preparation, career advice, and professionalism as it pertains to undergraduate STEM majors. Our panelists range from recent alumni employed by notable businesses such as Amazon and Apple to the more experienced Farhad Mafie CEO of Savant Company Inc. Please RSVP for an evening filled with meaningful conversations and opportunities for professional growth!
