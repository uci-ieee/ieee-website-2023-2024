How Can I Get Involved?

(It's Super Easy)

General Membership


Membership is as simple as showing up! Whether it's participating in one of our projects, attending a workshop, or just hanging out at a social, getting involved with us makes you a part of IEEE at UCI.

Regardless of your major or how often you attend events, you're always welcome to join us!


Member++


Member++ is a paid membership ($15/year) which grants you access to some special perks, including access to our 3D printers, ICs/MCUs, Reflow Ovens, and more. It also comes with a T-Shirt and a 15% DigiKey discount!


IEEE Global Organization


IEEE at UCI is a student branch of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, the world's largest professional society for all things electrical engineering. This worldwide organization has over 427,000 members throughout 190 countries, of which over 145,000 are students. IEEE publishes over 200 transactions, journals, and magazines, and puts together thousands of conferences every year. Getting involved with the Global Organization can help you to learn a great deal and kick start your career.

For more information, check out the Global IEEE website.
